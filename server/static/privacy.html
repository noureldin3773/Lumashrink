Privacy

Effective July 15, 2026

The short version: the macOS app processes files on your Mac. Files added to the free web trial are processed by the server running that trial and are deleted automatically within two hours or when you start a new session.

Files you process

The native LumaShrink app does not upload your media to LumaShrink. It reads and writes only the files and output folders you select. The web trial temporarily stores uploaded files to perform compression and download; those temporary session files are automatically removed within two hours.

Account and payment data

LumaShrink does not currently require an account. If you purchase Pro, the configured checkout provider processes payment and receipt information under its own privacy policy. LumaShrink does not receive or store full card numbers.

Diagnostics

The macOS app writes local diagnostic logs to your Mac so you can troubleshoot a failed run. These logs are not transmitted automatically. You choose whether to share them with support.

Analytics and cookies

This launch build does not include advertising trackers or cross-site analytics. The web trial uses an in-memory session identifier required to keep your queue working; it is not used for advertising.

Your choices

You may end a web session at any time by choosing New. This removes the temporary session files. You can remove native-app outputs and logs using Finder.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests can be sent through the support page.
