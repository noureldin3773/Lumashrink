## Executive Summary

LumaShrink was inspected across five public routes. The audit found and corrected missing form-control names, a keyboard-fragile upload control, a skipped footer heading level, insufficient text-token contrast, fixed preset-savings claims, and ambiguous desktop-versus-web privacy copy.

No remaining structural issue was found in the browser accessibility trees after remediation. This is not a WCAG conformance certification: Axe, Lighthouse, real screen-reader, 200% zoom, and physical keyboard testing remain outstanding.

After remediation, all five routes have a page title, English document language, one `main`, one `h1`, no duplicate IDs, no heading-level skips, and no positive `tabindex`. Every visible form control in the web trial exposes an accessible name, including the custom-size controls. Landing-page and app text tokens were darkened to clear the computed WCAG AA text-contrast threshold against their solid backgrounds. Primary-button gradients were reviewed separately because the lightweight checker cannot resolve gradient stops.

## Manual Testing Recommendations

- **Keyboard — SC 2.1.1, 2.1.2, 2.4.3, 2.4.7:** Using Safari and Chrome on macOS, traverse `/` and `/app` using only Tab, Shift+Tab, Enter, Space, and arrow keys. Verify the skip links, upload buttons, presets, selects, ranges, compression controls, and dialogs have logical order, no trap, and a visible focus ring.
- **Screen reader — SC 1.3.1, 2.4.6, 4.1.2, 4.1.3:** With VoiceOver, confirm page landmarks and headings, every compressor setting, queue updates, errors, progress, and batch completion. Status announcements need special attention because browser-tree inspection cannot prove live-region timing.
- **Visual — SC 1.4.3, 1.4.10, 1.4.11, 1.4.12:** Test 200% browser zoom, 320 CSS-pixel reflow, increased text spacing, High Contrast, and both light/dark system appearances. Recheck text placed over gradients and glass materials visually.
- **Motion/timing — SC 2.2.1, 2.2.2, 2.3.1:** Enable Reduce Motion and verify landing animations and smooth scrolling stop. Confirm no content flashes and the two-hour trial expiry never causes silent data loss while compression is active.
- **Cognitive/errors — SC 3.3.1–3.3.3:** Exercise unsupported formats, empty files, files above 50 MB, the three-file limit, unreachable targets, network interruption, and expired sessions. Confirm messages identify the problem and recovery action in plain language.
